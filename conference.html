Design is a serious business...

Interaction South America (ISA) is the most important conference about Interaction Design, User Experience, Technology and Innovation in South America.

Talks and discussions about technology, creativity, experience design, innovation and the design business will be present from November 18th to 21st of 2015 in Córdoba, Argentina.

The event first arrives to Córdoba, Argentina and will feature both nationally and internationally recognized speakers in an intensive program with lectures, workshops, academic papers, among other activities.

Thanks to the selection of top international speakers, it will be the largest and most important ISA to date. We also expect it to be considered the best design event of the year worldwide.

User Experience Design, Interaction Design and Human-Computer Interaction are broad terms that have been used by vibrant professional and academic communities, oftentimes in separate events in Latin America.

In 2015, we have decided to come together in Interaction South America and the Latin American Conference on Human-Computer Interaction.

During Days 1 and 2, a scientific track will present contributions selected by the CLIHC 2015 Program Committee, whereas a practitioners track will present work carefully curated by the ISA Professional Committee.

For the first time in South America, the synergy of both communities will be experienced.

  • 12 Keynote Speakers
    12 world-class speakers will talk about Interaction Design. The presentations will last 45 minutes with simultaneous translation in Portuguese and English.

  • +100 Talks
    Learn, get trained, update your skill-set, get to know what other professionals are doing and thinking. Lectures, Workshops, items, presented by their authors so that you know what is happening and about to happen in the world of interaction design in South America.

  • Networking
    During the 4-day event, we will have networking activities outside the conference schedule to achieve moments of relaxation and interaction among participants.

  • +600 People
    Everyone who is someone in interaction design will be present during 4 exciting days. If you are looking to contact the professionals who carry out discipline, looking for a change of air, or to get introduced in this amazing world, this is the perfect place to be.

  • Day 1 and 2

    The first two days of the event, November 18th and 19th, we will transform Cordoba's city center into a vibrant space dedicated to Interaction Design. With more than 100 academic articles, case market, lightning talks and workshops.

  • Day 3 and 4

    During the November 20th and 21st, great authorities of design, technology and the creative industry will lecture in Cordoba. There will be two exciting days to get in touch with the best. All with simultaneous translation, so you do not miss what they are here to tell.

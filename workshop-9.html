UX Strategy

Christian Rohrer

18 de Noviembre

09:00 - 13:00

English

Quiero inscribirme

In this all-day workshop, students will learn key lessons from the industry on these topics, have discussions on how it applies to their situation, and engage in small sgroup and individual exercises designed to help create your own personalized plan for making UX more strategic at their place of work.

Topics include:

  • Trends in business that directly affect user experience, such as the use of big data to drive decision making and executive reception to user research and design thinking
  • User Research methods available and techniques for measuring user experience.
  • Software Development Life Cycles and methodologies, such as the agile/lean movements and how they interact with
  • Strategic user experience techniques and their relevance to business and development practices, including Design Thinking, Personas, Guideline Development, and Content Strategy
  • The value of UX to different business models, including e-commerce, consumer advertising, subscription and enterprise B2B
  • Innovation approaches and the role of UX
  • Creating UX Organizations and teams
